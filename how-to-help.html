Found a Pet?

Here's What To Do

Five quick steps to help get them home safely

1
Stay Calm & Approach Slowly

Move slowly and speak softly. A scared pet that's chased is more likely to run further away.

2
Check for ID

Look for a collar, tag, or anything with an owner's name or phone number on it.

3
Get Them Scanned

Most vet clinics and shelters will scan for a microchip for free, no appointment needed.

4
Keep Them Safe

If you can safely contain them, keep them somewhere secure and away from traffic.

5
Report It Here

Submit a Found Pet report so our volunteer network — and the owner — can find you fast.
